(U//FOUO) Attend the Talk on WMD Proliferation -- Tomorrow (23 March)

FROM: International Affairs Institute
Run Date: 03/22/2006
(U//FOUO) The International Affairs Institute presents: Mr. Charles Curtis, COO of the Nuclear Threat Initiative (NTI).
(U//FOUO) Mr. Curtis will address the dangers of WMD proliferation on March 23, 2006 from 1030-1130 in the HQS Building, Room 9A135 conference room.
(U//FOUO) NTI is a non-profit organization working to reduce the global threats of nuclear, biological, and chemical weapons, and is co-chaired by Ted Turner and former Senator Sam Nunn.
(U//FOUO) Mr. Curtis has worked as the Under Secretary and later Deputy Secretary of the Department of Energy, where, among other things, he had direct programmatic responsibility for energy, science, technology, and national security programs. He was also recently Executive Vice President and COO of the UN Foundation, and is currently a member of the Council of Foreign Relations. Mr. Curtis plans to speak on the dangers of WMD (Weapons of Mass Destruction) proliferation.
(U) Please note that the presentation and Q\&A session will be at the UNCLASSIFIED level. Please join us for what promises to be a thought-provoking presentation and discussion on dangers of WMD proliferation.
