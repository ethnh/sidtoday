You are invited to visit the Conference's web site, review the agenda and sign up for sessions that are relevant and interesting to you:

# Client Collaboration Conference website 

(U//FOUO) This conference is intended for NSA clients, the Intelligence Community and the Department of Defense, as well as internal NSAers, and is specifically designed to promote collaboration, visibility, and feedback between NSA and external clients. This year's keynote speaker is SID's Deputy Director for Customer Relationships, RDML Tony L. Cothron, USN, and the conference will feature individual briefings, panel discussions, and breakout and training sessions.
(U//FOUO) Every effort will be made for internal NSA personnel to attend this conference; however, please keep in mind that seating is limited. As you register via the Event Registration System (ERS), you will be notified if you have reserved a seat or if the session is full.
