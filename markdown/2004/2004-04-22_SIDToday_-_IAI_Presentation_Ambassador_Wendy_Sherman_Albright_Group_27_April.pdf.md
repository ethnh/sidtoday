(U//FOUO) The International Affairs Institute (IAI) presents: "North Korea, Iran, and Beyond: Where in the World Are We?" by Ambassador Wendy Sherman, on 27 April 2004 from 10301130 in the Friedman Auditorium.
(U//FOUO) Ambassador Sherman served as Special Advisor to the President and Secretary of State and North Korea Policy Coordinator in the Clinton Administration. In this capacity, Ambassador Sherman was one of the principal negotiators of the 1994 Agreed Framework. Ambassador Sherman will discuss her experiences with North Korean leadership and her views on non-proliferation policy.
(U//FOUO) Please note that the speaker is not cleared, and that the presentation and question and answer session will be at the UNCLASSIFIED level. Please join us for what promises to be a thought-provoking presentation and discussion of North Korean leadership and non-proliferation policy.
